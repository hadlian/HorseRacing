## Converging structural finding

Each model's **near-miss slot is where the ROI lives**: R5 rank-3 (+17.4%) and CM rank-2
(+3.6%) both win at near-rank-1 rates at better prices, while both rank-1s sit at ≈ takeout.
The market absorbs each model's first choice (avg ML ~4.3-1) but underprices their second
tier. This is the strongest empirical input yet for the Phase 2 probability layer and
exotics construction: the system's edge, if it has one, is in ranks 2–3 contention, not in
win bets on rank 1 — and not in any confirmation-style filter, all of which failed ROI testing.
